## üé¨ Motion Language

### **Animation Principles**

- **Intentional:** Every motion serves a purpose
- **Precise:** Tight timing, no unnecessary movement
- **Elegant:** Smooth easing, natural feel
- **Performance:** GPU-accelerated transforms

### **Timing Functions**

- **Ease Out (Primary):** `cubic-bezier(0.16, 1, 0.3, 1)` ‚Äî Smooth, natural
- **Ease In Out:** `cubic-bezier(0.4, 0, 0.2, 1)` ‚Äî Standard transitions
- **Spring:** Natural bounce for interactive elements

### **Duration Standards**

- **Micro:** 150ms ‚Äî Hover states, quick feedback
- **Standard:** 300ms ‚Äî Standard transitions
- **Moderate:** 500ms ‚Äî Section transitions, card reveals
- **Slow:** 800ms ‚Äî Hero animations, major reveals

### **Motion Patterns**

- **Fade In + Slide Up:** Standard reveal
- **Scale + Fade:** Emphasis moments
- **Parallax Scroll:** Depth layers
- **Stagger Children:** Sequential reveals
- **Magnetic Hover:** Subtle attraction on interactive elements

## üéØ Interaction Model
