## üé¨ CINEMATIC MOTION LANGUAGE

### **Motion Philosophy: "Intentional Fluidity"**

Every motion serves the narrative. Nothing moves without purpose.

### **Animation Principles**

1. **Entrance Sequences**
   - Hero: Multi-stage reveal (background ‚Üí shapes ‚Üí text ‚Üí CTA)
   - Sections: Staggered reveals with parallax depth
   - Cards: Scale + fade + slight rotation for depth

2. **Scroll-Based Storytelling**
   - Parallax layers (background, midground, foreground)
   - Scroll-triggered animations (GSAP ScrollTrigger)
   - Progress indicators that show journey
   - Section transitions with color morphing

3. **Micro-Interactions**
   - Magnetic hover effects (subtle pull toward cursor)
   - Button ripples and glow pulses
   - Card lift with shadow expansion
   - Text reveal on scroll (split text animations)

4. **Advanced Techniques**
   - GSAP ScrollTrigger for complex scroll sequences
   - Framer Motion for component-level animations
   - CSS transforms for performance (GPU-accelerated)
   - Intersection Observer for viewport-based triggers

### **Timing & Easing**

- **Hero Entrance:** 1.2s, custom cubic-bezier (0.25, 0.46, 0.45, 0.94)
- **Section Reveals:** 0.8s, ease-out
- **Micro-interactions:** 0.3s, spring-like
- **Scroll Animations:** Continuous, smooth, 60fps
